About Adobo

Adobo is a popular Filipino dish made with meat (commonly chicken or pork) marinated in soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, and spices, then simmered until tender.

It's a delicious and flavorful dish enjoyed by many.

Recipe

  • Chicken
  • Soy Sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Garlic
  • Black Peppercorns
  • Bay Leaves
  • Cooking Oil
  1. Marinate the meat in soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, peppercorns, and bay leaves for at least an hour.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, then brown the meat.
  3. Pour in the marinade and simmer until the meat is tender.
  4. Serve with steamed rice and enjoy!
